SMTP TLS Reporting

This page collects SMTP TLS Reports (RFC8460) sent to smtp-tlsrpt@aykevl.nl. You can configure your own mail server to do the same by setting the rua field in your SMTP-TLSRPT DNS record to mailto:smtp-tlsrpt@aykevl.nl, for example:

_smtp._tls.aykevl.nl.  59  IN  TXT  "v=TLSRPTv1;rua=mailto:smtp-tlsrpt@aykevl.nl"

If you do this, some information about SMTP traffic to your domain will be publicly available and will be stored locally for up to 7 days. If you want to stop this, update the TXT record above to not include mailto:smtp-tlsrpt@aykevl.nl, which will remove all information from this server in about 8 days (depending on the speed of DNS propagation). Contact me if you want to have this information removed more quickly or you have other questions about this service.
